🎓 About Me

I am an undergraduate researcher at Jiangnan University (Project 211 & Double First-Class). My work spans computer vision—especially medical image segmentation and vision-language fusion—AI4Science (protein & small-molecule design), and large-language-model reasoning with neuro-symbolic agents.

I have 3 papers accepted (all as first / co-first author), spanning ICIC 2026 (×2 Oral) and ICML 2026, filed 1 invention patent, and won Gold at iGEM 2025 as the dry-lab (computational) lead.

I am actively seeking research opportunities (RA, summer internship, or graduate study) in computer vision, AI for Science, and LLM reasoning. Please feel free to reach out.

🌟 Research Vision

I want machines that not only perceive, but reason. I am driven by two complementary pursuits:

  • Seeing fine structure. Designing vision models—Mamba/SSM, wavelet, alignment-adaptive fusion—that segment fine structures under weak semantic prompts, and that stay robust to AI-generated content.
  • Reasoning machines for science. Building LLM-driven, neuro-symbolic agents and generative models for proteins and peptides that fuse symbolic reasoning with deep representation learning to solve concrete design problems.

🧪 Featured Projects

iGEM 2025 · Gold Medal · Dry-Lab Lead

AMP Forge — Antimicrobial Peptide De-Novo Design

A general AMP design platform tackling antibiotic resistance via the human peptide LL-37 expressed in S. cerevisiae. Built the largest curated AMP corpus and a three-stage pipeline: ESM-2 / ProtT5 / Ankh + BiGRU-VAE latent space → Latent Diffusion → Transformer decoder, trained MLE → RL adversarial → diffusion fine-tuning. Supports six generation modes and achieves SOTA on multiple metrics; generated variants outperformed the wild type in wet-lab assays.

PyTorchESM-2ProtT5Ankh Latent DiffusionBiGRU-VAERLHF
First Author · ICIC 2026 Oral

FWMamba-UNet — Medical Image Segmentation

A Mamba state-space UNet augmented with frequency-domain and wavelet-transform branches, capturing cross-scale boundary cues that pure spatial UNets miss, with consistent gains across multiple medical segmentation datasets.

First Author · ICIC 2026 Oral · Patent

Extract-Then-Compile: an LLM agent that lifts natural-language constraints into a symbolic program, then solves it reliably via classical search. Filed as a national invention patent.

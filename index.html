🎓 About Me

I am an undergraduate researcher at Jiangnan University (Project 211 & Double First-Class). My research focuses on computational chemistry and AI4Chem. During my undergraduate studies, I have followed two methodological threads—Mamba-based structural modeling and neuro-symbolic methods—and am bringing them together to build closed-loop scientific workflows.

I have 4 papers spanning ICML 2026, ICIC 2026 (×2 Oral), and PRCV 2025; filed 1 invention patent on reliable neuro-symbolic planning; and won Gold at iGEM 2025 as the dry-lab (computational) lead.

I welcome research collaboration, internships, and graduate-study conversations in computational chemistry, AI4Chem, and AI for Science.

🌟 Research Vision

My goal is scientific AI that can model long-range physical structure and act through verifiable reasoning. Two research threads form the foundation:

  • Mamba structural modeling. Extending state-space models from frequency-aware visual structure to linear-complexity modeling of long-range interactions in three-dimensional atomistic systems.
  • Neuro-symbolic scientific intelligence. Compiling constraints and scientific knowledge into deterministic, evidence-aware mechanisms so that agents can verify, repair, and revalidate actions before execution.

🧪 Research Project

iGEM 2025 · Gold Medal · Dry-Lab Lead

AMP Forge — Antimicrobial Peptide De-Novo Design

Our team engineered S. cerevisiae to produce the human antimicrobial peptide LL-37 against antibiotic resistance. I independently built AMP-Forge: protein language model → VAE compressed to a 64-D latent space → latent diffusion → non-autoregressive decoding. I curated 25,622 peptides from six databases and added an activity predictor (PCC = 0.90) for closed-loop screening. Multiple generated variants outperformed the original sequence in wet-lab validation.

A linear-complexity architecture for machine-learning interatomic potentials that represents charge transfer, electron delocalization, and periodic response as unified source–propagation–response state dynamics. Verifiable Scientific Agents

Chem-Agent · Verified Repair Contracts

A cognitive control layer for the silent failure mode where code runs but the science is wrong. VRC compiles scientific knowledge into executable contracts with applicability domains, evidence, and explicit unknown boundaries, then verifies tool calls, performs safe repairs, and reruns all checks before execution.
